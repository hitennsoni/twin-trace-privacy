Twin Trace — Account & Data Deletion

Developer: Hiten Soni · App: Twin Trace

Twin Trace lets you optionally sign in with your Google account or Play Games to back up your game progress to the cloud. We do not create a separate password-based account — sign-in uses your existing Google/Play Games identity. This page explains how to request deletion of the data we store for you.

How to request deletion

Email hitennsoni@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Twin Trace sign-in, with the subject “Delete my Twin Trace data”.

We will verify the request and delete your stored data. Requests are processed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your cloud-saved game document: level progress, coins, hints, achievements, daily-challenge history, and settings.
  • The account identifier we store to link that save to you.

What is kept (and for how long)

  • Nothing tied to your identity is retained after deletion, except where retention is required by law.
  • Anonymous crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) are not linked to your account and expire automatically per Google’s retention.

Delete data on your device

  1. In the app: Settings → Cloud Save → Sign out to stop syncing.
  2. Uninstalling Twin Trace removes all local game data from your device.
