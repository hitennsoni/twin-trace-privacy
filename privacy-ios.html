Twin Trace (iOS) — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Twin Trace (“we”, “the app”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the iOS app does — and doesn’t do — with your data. By using Twin Trace you agree to this policy.

The short version: Twin Trace does not collect any personal data. There is no account and no login. Your game progress stays on your device and, if you enable it, syncs privately through your own Apple iCloud. We have no server and cannot see your data.

Information we collect

None. The developer does not collect, store, or receive any personal information from you. There is no sign-in, no user profile, and no analytics or tracking.

Your game data

  • On your device. Your level progress, points, hints, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud sync (optional). If you use iCloud, your progress is synced across your own Apple devices through Apple’s iCloud. This data lives in your personal iCloud account — it is handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy, and the developer has no access to it.

Purchases

In-app purchases (points packs and the Pro upgrade) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Purchase handling is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

Advertising, analytics & tracking

Twin Trace contains no third-party advertising SDKs, no analytics, and no tracking. The app does not access your device advertising identifier (IDFA) and does not track you across apps or websites. The in-app “watch a video for points” feature is a simulated reward shown entirely within the app — it is not a real ad network and transmits no data.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data, because we do not collect any. The only data leaving your device is your own iCloud sync (to Apple) and App Store purchase processing (by Apple), both under Apple’s control.

Children

Twin Trace is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your choices

  • Play entirely offline — no connection is required.
  • Turn iCloud sync on or off in your device’s iCloud settings.
  • Delete the app at any time to remove its on-device data.

Changes
